Privacy Policy of Jenkins for BlackBerry 10

What information do we collect?
We do not collect any private or sensitive information in any central system when you use Jenkins for BlackBerry 10 application.

What do we use your information for?
Any of the information we collect from you is used to understand which versions of Jenkins for BlackBerry 10 are actively used and from which countries, using the source IP address of the phone and external geo-location services.
Should you decide to send your local activity log to our customer support e-mail, we use that information to understand and reproduce the problem on our test environments and improve Jenkins for BlackBerry 10 software in the next releases.

How do we protect your information?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your activity logs you decide to send to our customer support.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties ?
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your usage or activity logs.

Your Consent
By using Jenkins for BlackBerry 10 application, you consent to our privacy policy.

Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
